The Story of Goldie and the Three Bears

Our Hero, Goldie
This is the innocent child who happened upon the lair of the infamous Bears.

Goldie told us about her harrowing experience with the ferocious beasts in their own home. The daring child thumbed her nose at the furry critters by, among other things, claiming the rich vittles the bears had been hoarding, sitting in the overly-comfortable pieces of funiture in their hovel and even sleeping in their beds!

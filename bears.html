How dare that little thief come into a family's home uninvited, sit on their furniture, lie on their beds, and eat their food! Mr. Bear told us that when they returned from a walk their home had been ransacked and their kitchen violated. According to this unfortunate family, when they found Ms. Locks in the bed of the youngest bear, Baby, she terrified them in her haste to exit the dwelling. Mrs. Bear told us that both Baby and herself are just beside themselves with stress from the incident, and Baby feels the need to have one of the parents to check his room before he enters! Scandalous!
